# Counterfactual platform-learning / Trojan-horse analysis

Construct the strongest technically coherent mechanism by which a frontier provider could use a legal workflow product to acquire strategic leverage without obviously taking raw client files.

Test this chain:

1. Valuable legal productivity tool earns deployment and connector access.
2. Onboarding captures playbooks, risk appetite, escalation rules, seed agreements, and local terminology.
3. Routine use captures accepted/rejected outputs, exceptions, rationales, and outcomes.
4. The provider retains or derives structured signals.
5. Signals are normalized across organizations into a common legal ontology.
6. The provider improves generalized workflows, evaluation suites, routing, or vertical products.
7. The provider bundles adjacent legal work, weakens intermediaries, or creates switching costs.

For every step, classify repository evidence as Confirmed, Inferred, Unknown, Contradicted, or Not Applicable. Identify the exact additional evidence needed to move an Unknown step to Confirmed.

Then construct the strongest benign architecture using the same visible code. Compare explanatory power. End with a calibrated conclusion, not a verdict chosen in advance.
